# AI Contribution Policy

streamqueue accepts AI-assisted contributions under these conditions:

1. **Disclosure is mandatory.** In your PR description, include:
   ```
   ### AI Assistance
   Tool: [name]
   Used for: [specific tasks the AI helped with]
   Human-verified: [what you personally reviewed and tested]
   ```

2. **You must understand every line.** If a reviewer asks "why did you do X?",
   "my AI suggested it" is not an acceptable answer.

3. **Run all checks locally.** Do not rely on CI to validate your work.

Contributors who do not disclose AI usage will have their PRs closed and will
receive a warning. Repeat offenses result in a ban.
